All About the Website

This website aims to give insight into who I am and what I have achieved within the past four years at the University of Missouri- Columbia. It is easy to read something about someone on a piece of paper however, a visual aid such as my website can allow someone to see the bigger picture than just a resume. This site will showcase my personality and the specific details of my achievements. My goals for this site are to better my learning and skillset in HTML and CSS as well as learn about learning technologies and how they influence my field of study in Engineering Information Technology. My objectives for this site are to inform people about who I am professionally and personally, display information about myself in an easy-to-follow way through HTML and CSS and showcase the skills I have learned in this course through website aesthetics and good programming practices.
